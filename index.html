About MPA

Overview

The MPA is a prioritizing algorithm for Next Generation Sequencing molecular diagnosis. We propose an open source and free for academic user workflow.
Variant ranking is made with a unique score that takes into account curated database, biological assumptions, splicing predictions and the sum of various predictors for missense alterations. Annotations are made for exonic and splicing variants up to +300nt.
We show the pertinence of our clinical diagnosis approach with an updated evaluation of in silico prediction tools using DYSF, DMD, LMNA, NEB and TTN variants from the human expert-feeded Universal Mutation Database with courtesy regards of curators for pathogenic variants and from the ExAC database to define the dataset of neutral variants.
